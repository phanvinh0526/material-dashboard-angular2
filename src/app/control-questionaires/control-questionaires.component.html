Asthma control questionnaires

Questionnaire-based tools can be used to standardise review of asthma symptoms.

Primary care Asthma Control Screening tool (also known as Pharmacy Asthma Control Screening tool

A quick screening test to detect poor asthma control, developed and validated for use with Australian patients attending primary care

Good Control Asthma

Have you experienced any of the following more than the given timeframe? Yes No
Less than 2 times per week have Asthma symtoms at Daytime
Less than 1 time per week have athma symptoms at Night time which caused sleep disruption
None limitation in routine activities that can cause asthma symotoms
Using relivers less than 2 times per week to control asthma symptoms
FEV or Peak flow is greater than or equal 80%
Interpretation : ‘Yes’ to more than 4 questions ( 80% ) indicates that the person may have goo controlled asthma. Otherwise more detailed assessment is needed.
